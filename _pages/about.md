Hey there,

I am an AI researcher currently working at [Fujitsu Research India](https://global.fujitsu/en-apac/local/technology/research). My primary work involves investigating memory architectures for agentic LLM systems. I am also working on evaluating and improving LLM powered coding agents on long-horion tasks. Previously, I graduated from IIT Madras (2025) with a BTech. in Mechanical Engineering and MTech. in Data Science ([Wadhwani School of Data Science and AI](https://dsai.iitm.ac.in/)). I was fortunate to have been advised by [Prof. Balaraman Ravindran](https://dsai.iitm.ac.in/~ravi/) under whom I worked on evaluating restless multi-armed bandit systems for resource distribution in public health settings.
